Vincent & Kayla's Budget Worksheet

A starting scaffold — the category names are just suggestions, so rename, add, or delete anything. Set a target for each line (what you plan to spend), then fill in the actual as the month goes. Everything saves as you type.

How this works
  1. Rename the categories to match your life, and add any that are missing.
  2. Put a target next to each — your best guess at a monthly number.
  3. Fill in actuals as you spend. The worksheet shows where you're over or under.
  4. When it's filled in, tap Copy summary and send it back — your own live tool gets built from these numbers.

The Excel and PDF downloads need an internet connection (they fetch their tools the first time you tap them). The worksheet itself works offline once loaded.

Optional, and off unless you turn it on. When it's on, a small light on Papa's dashboard shows how your month is going — green, amber, or red — with percentages only (like “the plan uses 97% of income”), never your dollar amounts, categories, or line items. No messages, no emails — just the light. Switch it off any time and it goes dark.

Everything you type is saved on this device only — nothing is shared unless you tap Copy summary and send it, or switch on Papa's status light above (which publishes status percentages only, never your numbers). The target boxes are shaded; the actual boxes are white. Not financial advice — a planning worksheet to get your numbers in one place.
