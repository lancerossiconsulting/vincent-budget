Vincent & Kayla's Budget Worksheet

A starting scaffold — the category names are just suggestions, so rename, add, or delete anything. Set a target for each line (what you plan to spend), then fill in the actual as the month goes. Everything saves as you type.

How this works
  1. Rename the categories to match your life, and add any that are missing.
  2. Put a target next to each — your best guess at a monthly number.
  3. Fill in actuals as you spend. The worksheet shows where you're over or under.
  4. When it's filled in, tap Copy summary and send it back — your own live tool gets built from these numbers.
